Features

A walkthrough of every feature in LinkedIn AI Publisher — from one-click post discovery to fully automated campaigns.

💡 Content Discovery

Turn any text into LinkedIn post ideas. Paste your notes, a blog article, a research excerpt, or anything — AI reads it and suggests 6 ready-to-write post angles.

How it works

  1. 1
    Paste Content

    Paste any text in the input box — notes, articles, brainstorming, meeting notes, etc.

  2. 2
    Discover Ideas

    Click "Discover Content Ideas". AI extracts themes and expertise, generating 6 unique angles.

  3. 3
    Select Ideas

    Click any idea cards to select them. Each shows the concept, angle, hook, and hashtags.

  4. 4
    Pick a Tone & Generate

    Choose Professional, Conversational, Story-driven, Educational, or Inspirational. Click Generate.

  5. 5
    Review in Drafts

    Generated posts appear in Drafts ready for editing, approval, scheduling, or publishing.

Supported Input Types

  • Blog articles or website content (paste text)
  • Research papers or reports
  • Personal notes and brainstorming
  • Meeting summaries
  • Existing LinkedIn posts (to generate variations)
  • Any plain text

📖 I Read

Read an interesting article, paper, or blog post? Paste it and get 3 complete, ready-to-publish LinkedIn posts written in your voice — as if you're sharing your key takeaways with your network.

What makes it different

  • Posts are fully written (not just ideas) — ready to publish immediately
  • Written in first person: "I just read...", "This paper changed how I think about..."
  • 3 distinct angles: Key Insight Practical Application My Nuanced Take
  • All 3 pre-selected by default — save any or all to Drafts with one click
  • Saved directly as drafts (no generation queue — instant)

How it works

  1. 1
    Paste the Article

    Go to Content → I Read tab. Paste the article content. Choose a tone.

  2. 2
    Summarise & Write

    Click "Summarise & Write 3 Posts". AI reads the article and produces 3 complete LinkedIn posts.

  3. 3
    Review Posts

    Expand each post to read the full text. Toggle any you don't want. Each post shows its angle badge and hashtags.

  4. 4
    Save to Drafts

    Click "Save X Posts to Drafts". Posts appear in Drafts immediately — no waiting.

🎯 Campaigns

Define a content niche, set a recurring schedule, and let the AI automatically generate and publish LinkedIn posts on your behalf — on autopilot.

ℹ️

Key rule: A campaign's niche (industry, topics, keywords) is locked after creation. To change it, delete the campaign and create a new one. All other settings (name, tone, schedule, approval mode) are editable.

5-Step Campaign Wizard

Step 1 — Identity

Give your campaign a name and optional description. E.g. "Weekly AI Insights", "Leadership Thought Leadership".

Step 2 — Niche (Locked After Creation)

Select your industry, define the topics AI should write about, and optionally add keywords to incorporate.

⚠️

This step shows a warning banner reminding you that niche settings cannot be changed after creation.

Step 3 — Style

Choose a tone of voice and optionally paste 1–3 example LinkedIn posts you love. AI uses these to match your exact writing style.

Step 4 — Schedule

Weekly schedule
  • Click day chips: Mon, Tue, Wed…
  • Set a time (24h format)
  • Choose timezone
Monthly schedule
  • Click date chips: 1st, 5th, 15th…
  • Set a time (24h format)
  • Choose timezone

Step 5 — Approval Mode

⚡
Auto-post
Posts go directly to LinkedIn without any review. Fully hands-free.
✉️
Email Approval
You receive an email with the post preview. Go to Drafts to approve before it publishes.

Managing Campaigns

ActionWhat changes
▶ StartAdds a repeating BullMQ cron job. Posts will generate on schedule.
⏸ PauseRemoves the cron job. No new posts until reactivated.
✏️ EditChange name, tone, example posts, schedule, approval mode (not niche)
🗑️ DeleteRemoves campaign and cancels the cron job permanently

📝 Drafts & Approval Workflow

All generated and AI-written posts start as drafts. The Drafts page is your editorial queue.

Draft Statuses

StatusMeaning
draftNewly generated or saved — awaiting your action
pending_approvalCampaign generated post waiting for email approval
approvedApproved and queued for auto-publishing
scheduledScheduled for a specific future date/time
publishedSuccessfully posted to LinkedIn
rejectedRejected with a reason (not published)

Actions on a Draft

  • Edit content — Modify the post text or hashtags inline
  • Approve — Moves to approved status and queues for publishing
  • Publish Now — Immediately publishes to LinkedIn
  • Schedule — Pick a future date/time for automatic publishing
  • Reject — Marks as rejected with an optional reason
  • Delete — Permanently removes the draft

📊 Published Posts

View all posts that have been successfully published to LinkedIn. The Published page shows:

  • Full post content and hashtags
  • Publication date and method (manual / auto / scheduled / campaign)
  • Direct link to the LinkedIn post
  • LinkedIn engagement data (if available)

📅 Scheduled Posts

Schedule any approved draft for a specific future date and time. The BullMQ scheduler picks it up and publishes at the exact specified time (within ±1 minute).

🖼️ Media Library

Upload and manage images and videos to attach to LinkedIn posts. Media is processed and stored in-memory (no file system storage in Docker).

🤖 AI Provider Settings

Each user configures their own AI provider in Settings → AI Provider. API keys are encrypted with AES-256-GCM before being stored in MongoDB.

ℹ️

Ollama tip: When running Ollama on a local machine and the app in Docker, set the endpoint to http://host.docker.internal:11434 so the container can reach your host machine's Ollama instance.

Model Auto-Discovery

For all providers, available models are fetched automatically when you configure the provider — no need to type model names manually. For Ollama, the app queries /api/tags on your Ollama server to list installed models.

🔔 Notifications

In-app notification centre for all platform events:

  • Post published to LinkedIn (auto or manual)
  • Campaign generated a post requiring approval
  • Scheduled post published
  • Post generation failed
  • LinkedIn token refresh events

🛠️ Developer Mode

Enable Developer Mode in Settings to unlock two additional sidebar items:

System Logs

A real-time terminal-like interface streaming all server-side console.log / console.error / console.warn output. Logs are stored in Redis and displayed with timestamps and colour-coded severity.

Job Queues

A live view of all BullMQ queues — active jobs, waiting jobs, completed and failed jobs. Useful for debugging why a campaign didn't fire or a post didn't generate.

🛡️ Admin Panel

Admin users have access to an additional Admin section in the sidebar:

  • Admin Overview — Platform-wide stats (users, posts, LinkedIn accounts)
  • User Management — List all users, toggle active/suspended status, view activity
  • Activity Logs — Full audit log of all user actions across the platform
