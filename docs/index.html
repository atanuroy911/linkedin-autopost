LinkedIn AI Publisher

An open-source, self-hosted platform to automatically generate, schedule, and publish AI-powered content to LinkedIn — with full control over your data, AI provider, and posting workflow.

Get Started → Explore Features

What is LinkedIn AI Publisher?

LinkedIn AI Publisher is a self-hosted, full-stack web application that combines AI content generation with LinkedIn's publishing API. It helps professionals and founders maintain a consistent LinkedIn presence without spending hours writing posts manually.

You bring your own AI provider API key (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, Ollama, or OpenRouter), connect your LinkedIn account, and the platform handles content discovery, campaign scheduling, approval workflows, and publishing.

Key Features

💡
Content Discovery
Paste any text — notes, articles, blog posts. AI extracts 6 unique LinkedIn post angles.
📖
I Read
Paste an article you've read. AI writes 3 complete LinkedIn posts from different perspectives, in your voice.
🎯
Campaigns
Define a niche, set a weekly/monthly schedule, and let AI auto-generate and publish posts on autopilot.
✉️
Email Approval
Choose to review every post before it goes live. Get an email with a preview and approve from Drafts.
🤖
Multi-Provider AI
Works with OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, Ollama (local), and OpenRouter.
🔒
Self-Hosted
Your data stays on your server. All API keys are AES-256-GCM encrypted at rest.

Quick Start

  1. 1
    Clone & Install

    Clone the repository and install dependencies with npm install.

  2. 2
    Configure Environment

    Copy .env.example to .env.production and fill in MongoDB URI, Redis URL, LinkedIn OAuth credentials, and your encryption key.

  3. 3
    Deploy with Docker

    Run ./deploy.sh to build and start all containers (web server + BullMQ worker + Redis).

  4. 4
    Connect LinkedIn

    Log in, go to Settings → LinkedIn, and connect your account via OAuth.

  5. 5
    Configure AI Provider

    Go to Settings → AI Provider, choose OpenAI / Claude / Gemini / Ollama / OpenRouter, and enter your API key.

  6. 6
    Create Content or a Campaign

    Use Content → Discover for one-off posts, Content → I Read to repurpose articles, or Campaigns to schedule automated posting.
